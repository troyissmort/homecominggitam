Homecoming 2022

Homecoming is curated to strengthen and engage the alumni community. GITAM's effort through Homecoming 2019 (December 2019) and Homecoming 2.0 (January 2021) was to deepen our relationship with the alumni and convey our commitment to serving them in every possible way. Homecoming 2022 (December 2022) takes inspiration and learnings from its predecessors and aims to advance the purpose powerfully.
This year, we are inviting alumni who graduated between 2001 and 2007 from the schools of Engineering, Sciences, Management, and Dental.
